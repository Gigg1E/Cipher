Welcome to Cipher

Cipher is a privacy-first, terminal-friendly chat platform that puts full control in the hands of the user. Whether you're self-hosting your own server or connecting peer-to-peer, Cipher guarantees encrypted messaging, fast connection, and complete data sovereignty.

Born from the frustration of centralized platforms and invasive policies, Cipher is lightweight, extensible, and completely open-source. No unnecessary bloat, no constant updates ‚Äî just stable, secure communication for communities that care.
